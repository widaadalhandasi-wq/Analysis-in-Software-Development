4. Weighting and Estimation 

4.1 Story Point Estimation 

Story points measure complexity and effort. They consider: 

Technical difficulty 

Time required 

Risks and uncertainty 

4.2 Estimation Techniques 

Planning Poker: Team members vote using cards 

T-shirt Sizing: XS, S, M, L, XL 

Fibonacci Sequence: 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13... 

4.3 Weighting by Complexity, Priority, Business Value 

Assign story points based on complexity 

Set priority (High, Medium, Low) 

Business value based on impact on user or revenue
