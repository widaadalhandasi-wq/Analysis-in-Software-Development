✅ Hospital Management System 

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is designed to automate and streamline hospital operations, improve patient care, and optimize administrative workflows. It covers clinical, administrative, and financial functions in one unified system.

Key Goals:

Improve patient experience and accessibility

Enable doctors and staff to efficiently manage records and schedules

Automate billing, inventory, and reporting processes

Ensure data security and compliance with medical regulations
