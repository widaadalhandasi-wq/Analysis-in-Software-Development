1. Purpose

The Hotel Management System (HMS) automates hotel operations, improving efficiency, guest experience, and staff productivity. It manages reservations, billing, room service, events, and guest communications in a single platform.

Key Goals:

Simplify booking and check-in/check-out processes

Optimize room utilization and staff allocation

Provide seamless guest services and notifications

Streamline billing, loyalty programs, and reporting
