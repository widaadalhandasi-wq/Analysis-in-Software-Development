2. Product Backlog Development 

2.1 Definition 

A Product Backlog is a prioritized list of features, enhancements, bug fixes, and technical tasks needed to develop a product. It guides Scrum teams on what to build next. 

2.2 How to Create and Structure a Product Backlog 

Identify high-level features (epics) 

Break epics into user stories 

Prioritize based on business value 

Add acceptance criteria for clarity 

Continuously refine as project evolves
